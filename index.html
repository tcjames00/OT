Opt-Out: Some accounts may not report balance information due to the institution having an "Opt-Out" on file with the customer. This is not within our control, and we are unable to provide balance information if an opt-out is on file.

Credit Reports: Access to credit reports is available only with a signed authorization from the consumer, as required by federal law.
